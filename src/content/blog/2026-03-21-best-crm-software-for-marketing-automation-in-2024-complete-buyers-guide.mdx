## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the difference between CRM and marketing automation software?

CRM software focuses on managing customer relationships and sales processes, while marketing automation handles lead nurturing and campaign management. Integrated platforms combine both functionalities, providing unified customer data and seamless handoffs between marketing and sales teams, resulting in 36% higher customer retention rates.

### How much does CRM marketing automation software cost per month?

CRM marketing automation costs range from free tiers supporting basic functionality to enterprise plans exceeding $5,000 monthly. Most small businesses pay $50-500 per month for platforms like HubSpot or ActiveCampaign, while enterprise solutions like Salesforce typically cost $150-300 per user monthly plus additional fees for advanced features.

Essential CRM integrations include email marketing platforms, social media management tools, accounting software, customer support systems, and analytics platforms. ### What ROI can I expect from CRM marketing automation?

Businesses typically see 300-500% ROI from CRM marketing automation within 12 months, driven by improved lead conversion rates, reduced manual tasks, and better customer retention. HubSpot consistently receives the highest customer support ratings with 24/7 chat support, extensive knowledge base, and free training resources. CRM data migration requires careful planning, data cleansing, and testing to ensure accuracy and completeness.
